Welcome to the Flores Lab at Williams College! We use social psychology theories and methods to understand how people's political identities affect their attitudes and behaviors. Research assistants in the Flores Lab help with a range of research duties, including coding qualitative data, running lab experiments, conducting literature reviews, and thinking through experimental designs with Prof. Flores. I am excited to work with curious and motivated students who want to work with me on my ongoing projects, which you can read about on the Research tab above.
