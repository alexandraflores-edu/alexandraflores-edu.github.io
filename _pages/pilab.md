Welcome to the Politics and Identity Lab at Williams College! We use social psychological theories and methods to understand how political identities affect people's attitudes and behaviors. Research assistants in the PI Lab help with a range of research duties, including coding qualitative data, running lab experiments, conducting literature reviews, and thinking through experimental designs. I am excited to work with curious and motivated students who want to work with me on my ongoing projects, which you can read about on the Research tab above.
